WHO'S THIS GUY?

Well, I am a graphic designer currently residing in Portsmouth, NH. My main focus is design but I enjoy many other things such as playing games, listening to music, and playing the drums.

 

Being creative is my passion. Ever since I was young, I have always looked for creative solutions to every problem that came my way. For me, graphic design keeps that alive. It pushes my limits and helps me use the best of my ability to find the right solution.
